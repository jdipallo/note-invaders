Learning To Read Music One Galaxy At A Time...

The objective of Note Invaders is for the budding musician to learn to sight read and develop their musical ear by hearing notes and melodies in a fun interactive way.

Like the classic game, Space Invaders, users shoot down the appropriate note from their chosen song and melody. Each correct note garnishes 100 points. Incorrect notes will cost you 100 points.

Upon completion of the game, users have the opportunity not only to see the melody on a staff, but they can play it back for further study.

At the moment, Note Invaders supports 2 octaves, C3-C5 and the Treble Clef. Bass Clef and further octaves will be supported in the future.

Also to note, Note Invaders is supporting quarter notes (1/4) only. So, when a melody is played back, it may not be totally rhythmically accurate. This will also be added in the next version.
