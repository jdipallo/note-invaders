Learning To Read Music One Galaxy At A Time...

The objective of Note Invaders is for the budding musician to learn to sight read and develop their musical ear by hearing notes and melodies in a fun interactive way.

Like the classic game, Space Invaders, users will shoot down the appropriate note from their chosen song and melody. Each correct note garnishes 100 points.

Incorrect notes will cost you 100 points.

Upon successfully completion of the game, users have the opportunity not only see the melody on a staff, but they can play it back as well for further study.

Have Fun!!!
